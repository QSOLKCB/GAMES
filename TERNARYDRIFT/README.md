Ternary Drift

A deterministic 2D space-trading and combat sandbox whose living economy generates missions, faction conflicts, and an adaptive tracker soundtrack controlled by ternary state vectors.

It takes the good bones of Freelancer:

free flight;
trade;
bounty hunting;
factions;
reputation;
equipment upgrades;
jump routes;
stations and planets;
exploration;
salvage;
no cinematic campaign bloat.

And gives it a compact identity of its own.
