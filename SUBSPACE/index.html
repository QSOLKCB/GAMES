FLIGHT & COMBAT

W↑Forward thrust

Acceleration is inertial. Release thrust to coast.

S↓Reverse thrust

Slower than forward thrust, but invaluable for range control.

AD←→Rotate

Your weapons fire along the hull axis and inherit your velocity.

SPACECTRLPrimary gun

Low-cost direct fire. Lead moving targets.

XTABSecondary ordnance

Bomb, mine, EMP, cluster, or ricochet payload by hull.

SHIFTAfterburner

Exceeds normal speed while draining the shared energy bank.

QHull special

Unique active system. Watch its energy cost and cooldown.

RRepel charge

Pushes nearby ships and hostile projectiles away.

PESCPause

Freezes the local fixed-step simulation.
